Affiliate

2021-04-19 Do you like what Juventutem is about? Do you have a heart to serve other young adults, and the Church and world in general? Are you more than an hour distant from each of the existing affiliates? Are you and two (or more) friends ready to undertake the commitments? Do you have a priest-friend (or three) that can celebrate Masses, hear confessions, and otherwise assist the efforts of young adults?

Then consider affiliating with the Fœderatio Internationalis Juventutem.

Complete an application and send it to us.

PDF: Application form

The existing chapters are here to counsel, support, and otherwise cheerlead anyone who will join us in this glorious effort. For one thing, we recommend reading the international Charter before submitting an application. (PDF: English / French)

If you have any questions, please use our contact information and send them to us.
