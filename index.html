20th anniversary of Juventutem!

A youth movement already twenty years old!

@secretary May 10, 2024

It was on the feast of Our Lady Help of Christians, 24 May 2004, that the name “Juventutem” was first mentioned in the correspondence of the founders. Twenty years later, the lay founding members of our international youth movement are either married (the oldest ones may soon become grandparents!) or in religious or consecrated life. Meanwhile, younger ones continue to animate our groups in America and the UK.

We invite all former and current members and supporters to attend or unite in spirit with the:



SOLEMN HIGH MASS of THANKSGIVING

which the international chaplain of Juventutem Fr Armand de Malleray, FSSP will offer

on Friday 24th May 2024, 7:00pm

(Confessions from 6pm)

at the Oratory of St Mary Magdalene, 96 North Side Wandsworth Common SW18 2QU London, UK,

assisted by Fr Henry Whisenant as Deacon and Fr Neil Brett as Subdeacon.

Refreshments will follow.

See Facebook page for this event here: https://www.facebook.com/events/377258975295750/

Juventutem at WYD Lisbon.

@secretary May 10, 2024

World Youth Day: Lisbon 2-6 August 2023

Whereas the uncertainty affecting the celebration of the traditional Latin liturgy precludes participation on the same scale as at previous World Youth Days, Juventutem will be present in Lisbon. Adequate venue has been secured in the centre of the capital. Doctrinal and spiritual conferences, and liturgies, will be held daily. Please note that Juventutem will take no booking of any kind. Pilgrims must secure their travel, accommodation, meals and any such arrangements separately from Juventutem.

Schedule: Friday 4, Sat 5 and Sun 6 August

Friday 4th August 9:30am – 1:00pm
Calçada do Duque de Lafões 1, 1950-207 Lisbon
– 9:30am: Arrival
– 10:00am Talk: “You are what you listen to: Sacred vs Pop Music” by music expert Fr Evans FSSP
– 11:00am: Low Mass
– 12:00pm Talk: “Four Last Things – Death, Judgement, Heaven & Hell” by Fr de Malleray, FSSP
